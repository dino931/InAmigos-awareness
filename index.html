OUR STORY


Born During Crisis,

build for change


InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government. It has its base at Chhattisgarh. It holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability and tax-exempt benefits for donors.


Join Us in Making a Difference Together, we can create a more inclusive, compassionate and empowered society.

What We Do



Project Bachpanshala

Equips underprivileged children with basic school education, digital literacy, and life skills.

Project Udaan

Provides vocational training, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial support for rural women.

Focuses on animal welfare, which includes rescuing, protecting, and daily feeding of stray animals.
